Also known as the Waterfall model

Strengths-
	Simple clear structure
	End goal determined early	
	Easy to document during each phase
	
	
Weaknesses-
	Changes to scope or goals are very difficult to accommodate
	Delays testing until after completion
	Excludes the client during the process
	
	
Types of projects and environments it suits-
	Suited to a project where the requirements will not change
	A strict and structured development environment requiring extensive documentation
	
	
Critical Factors-
	Requirements do not change
	Plan is precise and funding is sufficient
